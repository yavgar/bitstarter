Find your sport-mates and the information you want for any sport and any place.

Have you ever looked for a partner to train with?
No matter what's your favorite sport we will help you find your sport soul-mates to train with or to compare your performance to.

Have you ever visited a new place and was inetrested in information about outdoors routes to jog or bike?
Whether you are home or away, we will give you all the information you need to continue your training routine and improve your performance.

First-hand information about the best places to train are always available on your smart-phone.

We are passionate about sport and about training with whomever is equally passionate.

We are building a unique social application that creates a comprehensive profile of your workout based on data transmitted from ant+/bluetooth enabled gadgets (garmins, 4iiii, nike, etc.). This profile includes your bio-metrics such as heart rate, and any other available information such as type of sport, time of day that you train, or your favorite bike path.
By using our application you will get recommendations that complement your training and if you so wish will connect you with people that share your training patterns.

Enrich your experience in a healthy and fun way.

FAQ

What devices are you supporting?
Currently we are supporting all ant+/bluetooth devices and are first building an iPhone app.
Is my information secure?
Yes! The security and privacy of your information is of the outmost importance to us and any personal information is kept confidential and is only accessible by you. Non-identified information, such as running routes, is used to continue to build or vast information-base, but of course only in an anonymous form.
